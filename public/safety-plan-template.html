Safety Plan Template

Use this page to name your warning signs, your first coping steps, and the people or places that help you stay safer. This page is static and does not auto-save.

How to use it

Keep this practical. One short line in each section is enough. If you are in immediate danger, call 911 or call or text 988 now.

  1. Warning signs I notice first
  2. One thing I can do on my own for the next 10 minutes
  3. A place or activity that lowers the intensity a little
  4. One person I can contact right away
  5. Numbers I want in reach 988 Lifeline, 911, trusted contact:
  6. One step to make the space safer right now

Keep it easy to find

Bookmark this page, print it, or copy the sections into a note you can reach quickly when thinking is harder.
